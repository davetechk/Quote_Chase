How it works

Set up in 2 minutes.
Runs forever after that.

You enter the quote once. QuoteChase handles everything else.

1
Enter the quote
After a client meeting or site visit, open QuoteChase on your phone. Enter the client name, email, phone, project description, and amount. Takes 60 seconds.
2
Quote is sent instantly
The client receives a clean, professional quote by email and SMS. It looks like it came directly from you.
3
Follow-ups fire automatically
If the client goes quiet, polite follow-up messages go out on day 2, 5, 10, and 20. Each one gets slightly firmer. You do nothing.
4
Client replies — you win the contract
The moment the client replies, or you mark the project Won or Lost from your dashboard, all follow-ups stop. No awkward over-chasing.
